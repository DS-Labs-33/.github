## 🚀 Who We Are

**DS Labs** is the engineering playground and product studio founded by **[Divy](https://github.com/divyviradiya2)** and **[Sahaj](https://github.com/sizwinz)**. We are a duo of driven developers focused on building **scalable, efficient, and monetizable** software solutions. From intelligent, production-ready AI systems to high-performance web applications, we turn abstract ideas into deployed reality. We don't just write code; we ship products.

> *“We aim to build projects that matter, master the stack, and achieve independence through engineering.”*

## 💎 Featured Projects

### 🤖 [Campus Assistant](https://github.com/DS-Labs-33/Campus-Assistant)
> *A language-agnostic AI chatbot for educational institutions that handles complex routine routing in 7+ regional Indian languages.*
* **Stack:** Python, FastAPI, Next.js, Google Gemini AI, LangChain, ChromaDB.
* **Highlights:** Production-ready Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline, zero-cost multilingual translation layer, and built-in human escalation logic.
* **Status:** 🏆 TechSprint Hackathon Entry

### ☕ [Blue Can Coffee](https://github.com/DS-Labs-33/Blue-Can-Coffee)
> *A premium, mobile-first digital experience for a specialty coffee brand.*
* **Stack:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
* **Highlights:** High-end glassmorphism UI, strict local SEO optimization with JSON-LD schema mapping, and smooth conversion routing.
* **Status:** 🟢 [Live Demo](https://bluecancoffee.vercel.app/)

### ☕ [Enso Coffee](https://github.com/DS-Labs-33/enso-coffee)
> *A sleek digital sanctuary for an artisanal specialty coffee house.*
* **Stack:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, AOS.
* **Highlights:** Highly responsive cinematic layout, interactive menu configurations, and localized location intelligence routing.
* **Status:** 🟢 [Live Demo](https://ensocoffee.vercel.app/)
